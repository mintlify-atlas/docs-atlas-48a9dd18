# Introduction to Procurement Calendar

Procurement Calendar is an advanced enterprise system designed specifically for **Cloro de Hidalgo S.A. de C.V.** to manage, track, and control the complete lifecycle of raw material requisitions, supplies, and logistics deliveries.

## What is Procurement Calendar?

Procurement Calendar optimizes the supply chain flow at Cloro de Hidalgo by enabling seamless communication between Purchasing, Laboratory, CEDIS (Distribution Centers), and external suppliers. The system ensures every supply arrives at the right time and in the proper form.

<Note>
This system is built with Next.js 15, Supabase, and features real-time updates, role-based access control, and comprehensive tracking capabilities.
</Note>

## Who Is This For?

Procurement Calendar is designed for internal teams at Cloro de Hidalgo:

- **Purchasing Coordinators** - Manage requisitions and supplier agreements
- **Laboratory Teams** - Validate technical specifications and monitor arrivals
- **CEDIS Personnel** - Confirm receipt and incoming inventory
- **Administrators** - Oversee users, catalogs, and system-wide operations
- **Consulta (View-Only)** - Monitor requisitions and deliveries without editing permissions

### Logistics Calendar

Visualize supply schedules with a dynamic calendar interface:

- **Weekly/Monthly Views** - Monitor reception workload visually
- **Color-Coded Status** - Instantly identify pending, urgent, confirmed, or completed deliveries
- **Direct Management** - Drag-and-drop or click to view details and adjust schedules

### Master Catalogs

Standardize critical information to prevent data entry errors:

- **Suppliers** - Unified directory of supply companies
- **Products** - Master inventory of raw materials and supplies
- **Presentations** - Container types, packaging, and cargo units
- **Units of Measure** - Standardized weights and volumes (kg, L, pieces, etc.)
- **Destinations** - Reception points (Cloro de Hidalgo plants, CEDIS)

## Technology Stack

Procurement Calendar is built with modern, enterprise-grade technologies:

- **Framework**: Next.js 15+ with React 19 and Server Actions
- **Backend/Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Realtime)
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth with role-based security
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4
- **UI Components**: Shadcn UI (built on Radix UI)
- **Forms**: React Hook Form + Zod validation
- **Data Tables**: TanStack Table v8
- **Calendar**: FullCalendar v6
