The **Laboratorio** (Laboratory) role provides view-only access to the procurement calendar, allowing quality control team members to prepare for incoming material testing.

## Laboratory Responsibilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Monitor Deliveries" icon="calendar-search">
    Track scheduled arrivals of materials requiring quality testing
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plan Testing" icon="flask">
    Prepare testing schedules based on confirmed delivery dates
  </Card>
  <Card title="Coordinate with Coordinators" icon="users">
    Communicate quality requirements for incoming materials
  </Card>
  <Card title="Review Material Details" icon="clipboard-list">
    Access product specifications and delivery information
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Permissions Overview

Laboratory users have **read-only** access:

- ✅ **View** calendar with all scheduled deliveries
- ✅ **View** requisition details (product, supplier, quantities, dates)
- ✅ **View** all catalog data (products, suppliers, etc.)
- ❌ Cannot create or edit requisitions
- ❌ Cannot modify catalog entries
- ❌ Cannot delete any data
- ❌ Cannot access admin or configuration settings

<Info>
  Laboratory team members can only see information - they cannot make changes. For updates, contact a coordinator or admin.
</Info>

## Daily Workflow

### Morning Planning Routine

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar">
    Navigate to **Dashboard > Calendario** to see all scheduled deliveries
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Today's Arrivals">
    Check all requisitions scheduled for today. Look for:
    - **Confirmado** (blue) - Confirmed deliveries that may arrive
    - **En Tránsito** (purple) - Materials currently being shipped
    - **Recibido** (green) - Materials that have arrived at CEDIS
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check This Week">
    Switch to **Week View** to see all deliveries for the next 5-7 days
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Identify Testing Requirements">
    Click on each relevant delivery to review:
    - Product name and description
    - Supplier information
    - Quantity and presentation
    - Any special comments or requirements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prepare Lab Schedule">
    Plan testing capacity based on expected arrivals and testing complexity
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using the Calendar View

### Understanding Status Colors

The calendar uses color-coding to show delivery status at a glance:

- 🟡 **Pendiente** (Amber) - Awaiting supplier confirmation (may change)
- 🔵 **Confirmado** (Blue) - Confirmed by supplier (reliable date)
- 🟠 **En Revisión** (Orange) - Under review (possible issues)
- 🟣 **En Tránsito** (Purple) - Shipment in transit (arriving soon)
- 🟢 **Recibido** (Green) - Delivered to warehouse (ready for testing)
- 🔴 **Cancelado** (Red) - Order cancelled

<Tip>
  Focus on **Confirmado** (blue) and **En Tránsito** (purple) statuses for your testing schedule - these are most likely to arrive as planned.
</Tip>

### Viewing Delivery Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Event">
    Click on any colored event block in the calendar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Information">
    The detail modal shows:
    - **Product**: Material name (e.g., "Sosa Cáustica", "LABSA")
    - **Supplier**: Company providing the material
    - **Date**: Confirmed delivery date
    - **Quantity**: Amount and unit (e.g., 500 kg, 200 L)
    - **Presentation**: Package type (drum, bag, bulk, etc.)
    - **Destination**: Delivery location
    - **Status**: Current order status
    - **Purchase Order**: Reference number if available
    - **Comments**: Special instructions or notes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Note Testing Requirements">
    Review comments section for any specific quality control requirements mentioned by the coordinator
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close Modal">
    Click **Cerrar** to return to calendar view
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You will not see **Editar** (Edit) or **Delete** buttons in the detail modal - these are only available to coordinators and admins.
</Note>

## Weekly Planning

### Monday Morning Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to Week View">
    Click **Semana** in the calendar controls to see the weekly schedule
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="List This Week's Arrivals">
    Create a list of all materials scheduled for delivery this week
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Categorize by Priority">
    Identify which materials are:
    - Critical for production (test immediately)
    - Standard materials (regular testing schedule)
    - New suppliers (may require additional validation)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Testing Capacity">
    Ensure lab has capacity and reagents needed for expected tests
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coordinate with Team">
    Assign testing responsibilities to lab team members
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Daily Check-In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Morning Review (9 AM)">
    Check calendar for today's expected arrivals and confirm lab is ready
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Midday Update (1 PM)">
    Review if any statuses changed to "Recibido" (green) - materials arrived at warehouse
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="End of Day (5 PM)">
    Check tomorrow's schedule and prepare for next day's arrivals
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Coordinating with Other Teams

### Working with Coordinators

When you need information or have concerns:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the Issue">
    Note the specific requisition (product, supplier, date) you have questions about
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Coordinator">
    Reach out to the procurement coordinator via email or phone with:
    - Product and supplier name
    - Scheduled delivery date
    - Your specific question or concern
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Calendar Update">
    Ask coordinator to add special testing requirements in the **Comentarios** field
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Working with CEDIS

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share Testing Schedule">
    Inform warehouse team which deliveries require immediate testing upon arrival
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Sample Notification">
    Ask CEDIS to notify lab when materials marked for testing arrive
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coordinate Sample Collection">
    Arrange timing for collecting samples from warehouse to lab
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Use Cases

### Planning for New Supplier Materials

When you see a delivery from an unfamiliar supplier:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Supplier Name">
    Open the requisition details to see the supplier name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Product Specs">
    Note the product, quantity, and presentation details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prepare Enhanced Testing">
    Plan for more comprehensive testing protocol for first-time suppliers
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Allocate Extra Time">
    Schedule additional time for potential re-testing or validation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Coordinator">
    Ask if there are any special quality requirements or certifications needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing High-Volume Weeks

When the calendar shows multiple deliveries on the same days:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Clusters">
    Note which days have 3+ deliveries scheduled
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prioritize Testing">
    Determine which materials are most critical for production
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Coordination">
    Contact coordinator to see if any deliveries can be rescheduled
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prepare Resources">
    Ensure adequate staff, equipment, and reagents are available
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Expectations">
    Communicate testing turnaround times to production team
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tracking Delayed Deliveries

If you prepared for a delivery that didn't arrive:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Calendar Update">
    Refresh the calendar to see if the delivery date was changed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Look for Status Change">
    Check if status changed from "Confirmado" to "Pendiente" or "En Revisión"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Coordinator">
    If calendar doesn't show an update, reach out for current status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Schedule">
    Re-plan your testing schedule based on new information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reporting Issues

If you notice any problems with calendar data:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Document the Issue">
    Note what appears incorrect (wrong date, missing information, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Coordinator">
    Reach out to procurement coordinator with specific details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Context">
    Explain how the issue affects your testing schedule
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Suggest Solution">
    If applicable, recommend what information should be corrected
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Do not rely solely on the calendar for critical testing schedules. Always maintain direct communication with CEDIS and coordinators for high-priority materials.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why can't I edit any information?**

A: Laboratory role has view-only access by design. Contact a coordinator or admin to make changes.

**Q: How do I request additional information in the comments field?**

A: Contact the procurement coordinator and ask them to update the requisition with specific testing requirements.

**Q: What if the calendar shows a delivery but nothing arrives?**

A: Deliveries can be delayed or rescheduled. Contact the coordinator for current status - they may not have updated the system yet.

**Q: Can I export the calendar for my own planning?**

A: Currently, direct export is not available to view-only roles. Contact an admin if you need data exports for reporting.
