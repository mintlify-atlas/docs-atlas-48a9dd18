## User Management

### Approving New Users

When new team members register, they appear in the **Solicitudes Pendientes** (Pending Requests) section.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Administration">
    Go to **Dashboard > Administración** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Pending Requests">
    View all users with "pendiente" status awaiting approval
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Click **Aprobar** and select the appropriate role:
    - **Admin** - Full system access
    - **Coordinadora** - Create/edit requisitions and manage catalogs
    - **Laboratorio** - View-only access for quality control team
    - **CEDIS** - View-only access for warehouse team
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Approval">
    Click **Confirmar aprobación** to activate the user account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Users with "pendiente" status cannot access the system until an admin approves their account.
</Note>

### Managing Active Users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to Active Users Tab">
    Click the **Usuarios Activos** tab in the Administration panel
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Change User Roles">
    Click **Cambiar** next to any user to reassign their role
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove Users (Optional)">
    Use the trash icon to permanently delete user accounts
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a user account is permanent and cannot be undone. The user will need to register again to regain access.
</Warning>

## Catalog Management

### Managing Catalogs

Admins have full control over all system catalogs used in requisitions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Catalogs">
    Navigate to **Dashboard > Catálogos**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Catalog Type">
    Choose from the available tabs:
    - **Proveedores** (Suppliers)
    - **Productos** (Products)
    - **Presentaciones** (Package types)
    - **Destinos** (Delivery destinations)
    - **Estatus** (Status options)
    - **Unidades** (Units of measure)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Entries">
    Click **Nuevo [Type]** to create a new catalog entry
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Existing Entries">
    Use the pencil icon to modify existing catalog items
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Active Status">
    Click the status badge to activate/deactivate entries without deleting them
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete Entries (Admin Only)">
    Use the trash icon to permanently remove catalog items
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Toggle **Ver Inactivos** to show inactive catalog entries. Deactivating is safer than deleting when entries have existing dependencies.
</Tip>

### Status Colors

When managing the **Estatus** catalog, you can customize the color (hex code) for each status. This affects how requisitions appear on the calendar:

- **Pendiente** - Amber (#F59E0B)
- **Confirmado** - Blue (#3B82F6)
- **En Tránsito** - Purple (#8B5CF6)
- **Recibido** - Green (#10B981)
- **Cancelado** - Red (#EF4444)
- **En Revisión** - Orange (#F97316)

## Requisition Management

### Creating Requisitions

Admins can create requisitions on behalf of any department.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Requisition">
    Navigate to **Dashboard > Administrar Requisiciones** and click **Nueva Requisición**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Required Fields">
    Complete all mandatory fields marked with asterisks (*):
    - Fecha Confirmada (confirmed delivery date)
    - Proveedor (supplier)
    - Producto (product)
    - Presentación (package type)
    - Destino (destination)
    - Estatus (status)
    - Cantidad y Unidad (quantity and unit)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Optional Details">
    Fill in tracking information:
    - № REQUI (requisition number)
    - Fecha de OC (purchase order date)
    - Folio OC (purchase order number)
    - Fecha Solicitada (requested date)
    - Factura/Remisión (invoice/receipt)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Delivery Information">
    Update actual delivery details:
    - Fecha Entregado (actual delivery date)
    - Cantidad Entregada (actual quantity received)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Requisition">
    Click **Generar Nueva Requisición** to save
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Requisitions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Requisition">
    Find the requisition in the calendar or requisitions list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Details">
    Click the event to open the detail modal
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit">
    Click **Editar** to modify any field
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios** to update the requisition
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  All changes are tracked in the audit history (requisiciones_historial table) for accountability.
</Info>

### Deleting Requisitions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Requisition Detail">
    Click on the requisition event to open the detail modal
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Delete">
    Click the trash icon at the bottom left
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click **Sí, borrar** to permanently delete the requisition
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Only admins and coordinators can delete requisitions. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Calendar View

Admins can view and manage all requisitions in the calendar view.

- **Month View**: See all scheduled deliveries by month
- **Week View**: Switch to weekly view for detailed planning
- **Event Colors**: Each requisition is color-coded by status
- **Click Events**: Click any event to view details or edit

## Quick Add Feature

When creating requisitions, admins can quickly add missing catalog entries without leaving the form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Plus Icon">
    While creating a requisition, click the **+** icon next to any dropdown field
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Entry">
    Enter the name of the new supplier, product, or other catalog item
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save and Select">
    The new entry is automatically saved and selected in the form
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  This feature is especially useful when coordinators contact you about urgent orders from new suppliers.
</Tip>

## Common Admin Tasks

### Daily
- Review and approve pending user registrations
- Monitor calendar for delivery confirmations
- Respond to coordinator requests for catalog updates

### Weekly
- Review active users and role assignments
- Clean up inactive catalog entries
- Export data for management reporting

### Monthly
- Audit system usage and permissions
- Review and update status colors and categories
- Plan system maintenance or upgrades
